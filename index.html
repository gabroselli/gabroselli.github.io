background

Currently at SimplePractice as a Staff Engineer in the Revenue Cycle Management space.

Before that, I spent 12 years at Salesforce, where I worked across engineering and infrastructure teams operating at significant scale.

I started as a full-stack engineer, building Ruby on Rails applications and getting comfortable across the whole web stack. Over time I gravitated toward the backend and infrastructure, working on recommendation engines, analytics systems, and the operational layers that kept them running reliably at scale.

That trajectory pulled me naturally into infrastructure and platform engineering, where the problems are broader and the impact compounds across every team that builds on top of what you ship. Having worked deeply on both the product and platform sides, I'm equally comfortable partnering with Product on customer-facing features as I am designing the systems that make those features possible.

what i do

Software Engineering. I partner closely with Product to understand customer needs and translate them into reliable, well-crafted software. My focus is on shipping features that make a meaningful difference in the customer experience.

Platform Engineering. I build and maintain the foundational systems other engineers depend on, including cloud infrastructure, deployment pipelines, and internal tooling that keeps teams moving independently.

Security & Trust. I treat security as a design constraint, not an afterthought. From compliance certifications to service hardening and incident response, I work to make security a shared engineering responsibility rather than a gatekeeping function.

Technical Leadership. I operate across the individual contributor and leadership spectrum, guiding architectural decisions, mentoring teams, and advocating for simplicity when complexity starts to accumulate for its own sake.
